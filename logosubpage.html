I chose to use a circular shape as the base for my logo so that I can seamlessly incorporate it to social media pages as the profile image. The font used is Abril Fatface, a retro typeface found in many vintage advertisements. The colors used are earthy greens that further solidify the message of the brand, Plants of Quarantine.
